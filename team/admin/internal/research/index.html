Summer Schools (2%)

You are highly recommended to attend one of the summer schools at the first few years of your PhD. Please discuss this with Shadi.

  • Machine Learning Summer Schools
  • Medical Imaging Summer School – Biennial
  • International Computer Vision Summer School
  • Schools endorsed by MICCAI and BMVA are welcome.

Mentorship (3%)

You are strongly encouraged to mentor Bachelor and Master students. If you have a nice idea, please discuss it with Shadi, and write it down on this template, before you announce it on our student webpage. Alternatively, you can participate in grand-challenges as well.

Grand-Challenges (3%)

You are also recommended to participate in Grand-Challenges with your students. Interdisciplinary Projects offered at TU Munich is the perfect fit here. Most of the time, a publication with +30 co-authors ends up in a highly-impacted journal with a lot of citations. You only need to be on the top 10 on the leaderboard.

  • Federated Tumor Segmentation (FeTS) Initiative
  • Grand-Challenges in Medical Imaging

Resources and Guidelines for reading, writing and submitting Papers

One of the first steps in conducting your research is reading literature. However, how to read, quickly, efficiently, and seriously, a scientific paper?. The questions is addressed in the following resources:

  • How to Read a Paper
  • How to Read a Research Paper
  • How to Read Scientific Papers Quickly & Efficiently

Paper (10%)

  1. Read the following resourses:
    • Publish like a pro
    • How to write a first class paper
    • Heuristics for Scientific Writing
    • Guidelines for Wirting a Good NIPS Paper
  2. Read carefully the “Authors Guidelines” appaared in the corresponding conference’s or journal’s website. For example, pay attention on the format, page limit, anonymity, reference style, and special requirements.
