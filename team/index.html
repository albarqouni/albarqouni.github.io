Open Ph.D. Positions

There are many ways to fund your PhD position. I am listing here a couple of them:

  • New Openings by early next year 2022 (Please follow us on LinkedIn/ Twitter)

  • Positions (Have a look at the career page at Helmholtz AI).

  • If you are already hired in Industry and would like to do your PhD in one of our research themes, please drop me an email with your resume and motivation letter. This can be arranged case by case.

  • If you have already (or eligible for) third-party funding, e.g., DAAD, Marie-Curie Fellowship, and would like to do your PhD in one of our research themes, please drop me an email with your resume and motivation letter. The earlier you are in the process, the better we could help and support.
