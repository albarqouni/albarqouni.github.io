Presentation Structure

  • Introduction: In the first few slides, you need to introduce the subject to the audience. A brief background (big picture) and a few related works (more concise) would help you to position your paper in the big picture. It is quite important to talk about the key conclusions at the very beginning. The rationale for the paper (why you did the work?) has to be addressed by the end of the Intro. slides.
