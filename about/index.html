Research Interest

I am a Senior Researcher in the chair for Computer Aided Medical Procedures and Augmented Reality (CAMPAR) at Technical University Munich (TUM). My research lies at the intersection of human-computer interaction (HCI) and machine learning (ML). In particular, I develop tools to support both ML practitioner and end-user interaction (i.e. clinicians) with machine learning systems. Examples include developing predictive models from medical imaging leveraging the heterogeneous EHR data.
